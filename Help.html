Non-Politcal

R.T.I India is a non-political organization and takes the responsibility of educating the masses about the Right To Information Act which can be used by any Indian to get the desired information from the government

Non-Profit

R.T.I India is not operating for the profit or gain of its individual members.our purpose is only to help or educate peoples free of any cost.
